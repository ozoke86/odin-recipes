Bacon Wrapped Burger Bites

Description

These bacon wrapped cheeseburger bites, made in the air fryer,

are a little bit meatloaf and a little bit cheeseburger:

seasoned ground beef meatballs with Cheddar cheese centers are wrapped in bacon and fried.

They are delicious and reheat well, great as an appetizer or a main.

Serve with any dipping sauce you like.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1.5 tablespoons Italian-seasoned breadcrumbs
  • 1 large egg
  • .5 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon yellow mustard
  • 4 dill pickles, chopped
  • 4 ounces cheddar cheese, cut into half inch cubes
  • 9 slices bacon
  • 1 teaspoon sesamne seeds, or as needed for garnish

Directions

  1. Preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).

  2. Combine ground beef, breadcrumbs, egg, onion powder, garlic powder, parsley flakes, salt,

    ketchup, mustard, and dill pickle in a bowl and mix until just combined.

  3. Pinch 1 generous tablespoon of meat mixture and wrap around 1 cube of cheese. Roll to form a

    ball, then place on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Continue with remaining meat

    mixture and cheese.

  4. Cut bacon slices in half widthwise, then cut each piece in half lengthwise, making 4 strips

    from each bacon slice.

  5. Wrap 1 bacon strip around each meatball, securing with a toothpick or pressed tightly into

    the meat to stay in place. Any extra strips of bacon can be used to do a second wrap on some

    of the meatballs.

  6. Line the bottom of the air fryer with half of the meatballs, then fry for 6 minutes. Turn

    them over and fry for another six minutes. Remove and repeat with remaining meatballs.

  7. Sprinkle with sesame seeds. Serve with your choice of dipping sauce.
